GameplayCapture

Capture Your Gameplay

An Optimized Recorder for Your Gameplays

The program capture video and audio of your desktop and is optimized for gameplay capture. The numpad key decimal (.) is used to start the capture, the numpad key 0 is used to stop it. Let's see...

Considerations About the Program

It's easy to use. I also made a tool to print screen named PrintScreen simply, in a optimized way also. The print screen key is used for taking screenshots. It's better to use GeForce Experience if you have a Nvidia card for recording desktop. In this case, lower to minimum the audio quality from audio card settings, to not have unsynchronisation of audio and video.
